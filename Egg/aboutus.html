The Eggsperts

We just love eggs! We are a small company dedicated to providing quality eggs from a variety of wonderful birds. We understand nutrition, culinary arts, farming and therefore, eggs! We think the egg came first, because eggs are the source of life, we can all benefit from these wonderful round pieces of magic. Humans have been consuming eggs since the dawn of time, why stop now? Our eggs are very special because every step of the process is taken care of with love, a love you can even taste!
